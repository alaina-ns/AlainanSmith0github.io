SQL Car Dealership
Creating a database

The car dealership database includes entities for customers, sales, vehicles, salespeople, dealership, and insurance. Customers are the primary buyers of vehicles, providing essential information for the dealership. Sales records transactions between the dealership and customers, enabling revenue tracking and sales pattern analysis. Salespeople are vital for customer interaction, sales facilitation, and building relationships, and their information and performance contribute to the dealership's success. The dealership entity contains location, contact, and financial details. Additionally, insurance information is tracked for vehicles sold.
